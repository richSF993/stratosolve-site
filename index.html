Common Questions

StratoSolve is a new company — should that give me pause?
StratoSolve LLC was established in 2025. The expertise behind it is not new — Richard brings 12+ years of enterprise architecture, deployment, and advisory experience at 8x8. The company is new. The practitioner is not. For buyers who prioritize direct senior access over firm pedigree, that's an advantage.
How is StratoSolve different from a vendor-funded advisor?
Vendor-funded advisors earn compensation from the vendors they recommend — creating structural incentive misalignment. StratoSolve is retained directly by clients with no vendor quotas and no commission structures. Our only incentive is your outcome.
Can StratoSolve pass enterprise procurement review?
Yes. StratoSolve Technologies LLC is a registered California LLC with a federal EIN on file. W-9 documentation and standard vendor onboarding materials are available immediately upon request.
What does a typical engagement look like?
Every engagement begins with a no-cost 30-minute diagnostic — no pitch, no deck. From there, we scope around your specific challenges, typically beginning with a technology posture assessment before moving into architecture and execution.
Do you work alongside our existing IT team?
Yes — most engagements involve partnering with an internal team, not replacing one. We handle the strategic and cross-functional complexity that internal teams are often too operationally consumed to own at the level the engagement demands.
Can you work with our existing vendor relationships?
Absolutely. We are platform-agnostic and routinely optimize within existing vendor relationships — maximizing value from platforms already in place while being direct when a change is genuinely in your interest rather than ours.
